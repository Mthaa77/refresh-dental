## 16. ATHENA PAYMENT PLAN — FULL DETAILS

Athena is a South African dental finance platform. Key verified terms:

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Interest** | No interest — truly interest-free |
| **Fee** | One-time 6% processing fee on the total procedure cost |
| **Repayment options** | 3 months (4 instalments) or 6 months (7 instalments) |
| **Eligibility** | Credit and affordability check required |
| **Payment methods** | EFT, Snapscan, Masterpass, direct card payment |
| **Platform** | Powered by Paystack |
| **Missed payment penalty** | 5% fee if not settled within 3 days of missed date |
| **Athena website** | www.get-athena.co |

> ⚡ **Builder note:** Put this on a dedicated "Financing" section or "Payment Options" page. South Africans are price-sensitive about dental work — showing a payment calculator ("R5,000 implant = R893/month over 6 months") could meaningfully increase conversions.
